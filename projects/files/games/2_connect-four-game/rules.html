Rules

Objective

Be the first player to connect 4 of the same color discs in a row (either vertically , horizontally or diagonally)

How to play

  1. Red goes first in the first game
  2. Players must alternate turns , and only one disc can be dropped in one turn.
  3. Game ends one there is 4-in-a-row or stalemate .
  4. The starter of the previous game goes second on the next game.
